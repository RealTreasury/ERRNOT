The ERR NOT™ Method Explained

E - Education & Early Planning

  • Build the business case and obtain buy-in from management.
  • Gain understanding of the marketplace and plan key process steps.
  • Account for all licensing, implementation, and ongoing costs.

R - Requirements Gathering

  • Review and document your current state operations.
  • Determine and document all "must-have" requirements.
  • Rank and prioritize your top requirements to guide selection.

R - RFI/RFP

  • Develop a brief RFI focused on non-functional items.
  • Include vendor stability, market presence, and support.
  • Build a vendor shortlist based on pricing and initial demos.

N - uNique Demo Approach

  • Create a custom demo script to deliver to all vendors.
  • Keep your unique requirements front and center.
  • Use the script to highlight product strengths and weaknesses.

O - Obvious Winner

  • Remain unbiased and don't get distracted by flashy software.
  • Use a scoring process to eliminate confusion after demos.
  • Allow the data to highlight the obvious winning solution.

T - Transformation

  • Move to best practices to fully capitalize on new processes.
  • Manage project risks like data migration and change management.
  • Report on success metrics like ROI, time saved, and adoption.
  • Conduct regular system reviews and plan for upgrades.
